Who We Are

SlothWerks is a web application and multi-media consultancy in Grand Rapids. For two decades we have provided technical services and solid leadership to businesses and organizations throughout West Michigan. We bring to the table a passion for user-centered design, leveraging code and communication to craft solutions that will improve the lives of those living in our state and our world.

Stuff We’ve Done

The Locker Room Legacy

The Locker Room Legacy is a unique experience which combines aspects of social media, professional networking, mentoring, and event promotion. The platform directly benefits school sports programs as well as their current and former athletes. SlothWerks helped the Locker Room Legacy team with tutorial videos in order to streamline the user experience.

Kyros Digital

SlothWerks provides user experience research and product development services to Kyros Digital. Among the several projects we have worked on with Kyros are Banyan Tree Fabworks, Banyan Surplus, and the Kyros Digital website itself.

Habitrade

As a part of Grand Rapids Startup Weekend, SlothWerks provided UX research, UI wireframe design, and user flow expertise to a small team of developers, entrepreneurs, and creatives. The brief project resulted in an app concept that utilized both monetary and social components to incentivize the user to form good habits and stick to a multi-week “habit campaign”.

TownFly

Built in less than two weeks and launched at Start Garden in December 2017, the TownFly web application collected local events from Facebook based on location and other user input. The app was built by a team of four using the React JavaScript framework with a Node.js backend. Recent Facebook policy changes, unfortunately, rendered the API inoperative.

Stuff We’re Doing

Grand Circus

SlothWerks proudly supports the work of Grand Circus, a Michigan-based tech training program. We are the co-organizer of the local alumni meetup and assist with Grand Circus’ Intro to Code workshops, providing teaching assistance and social media promotion.

Loop Coding Center

SlothWerks is passionate about the education of our future leaders in Grand Rapids. At the Loop Coding Center, we offer assistance with course content, instruction, and program development.

Entrepreneurial Gold Podcast

Aurikk Brand Movement Group offers monthly nuggets of wisdom through its podcast, Entrepreneurial Gold. Throughout 2018, SlothWerks has provided editing and mixdown services for this inspiring and thought-provoking production.

The National Multiple Sclerosis Society

SlothWerks provides local support for the National Multiple Sclerosis Society via Team N3kk1d. This award-winning fundraising group handles everything from volunteer coordination to special event management for the MS Society and is responsible for more than $80,000 in donations for MS-related programs and research. In 2018 we tackled a wide range of services including website maintenance, live sound production, and event photography.
